Countries that Both Produce and Consume Coffee


World

In this visualization, the top plot is comparing the production vs consumption of each continent. The units of measure in the top chart are in units per one million people. To get an understanding of how much each continent is producing and consumer per capita (times one million) hover over the each globe that shows the corresponding continent you wish to view. At the bottom of the page, you can toggle the through the years. The bottom chart is showing the overall production and consumption each continent.

Continents

These visualizations are a further breakdown of each continent and show individual countries. Hover over the flags to see how much each country is producing and consuming. In the world view, there is a Oceania globe; however, it is not included in the later breakdowns because it covered only one country which contributed to very little production and consumption. To view the other continents, click here to start with Africa.
