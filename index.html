Objective and Datasets

Objective

With these visualizations we would like to shed light on quality, quantity, and equity factors across different regions for the benefit of coffee producers, enthusiasts, investors, and policymakers. Through analysis and visualizations of the dimensions of coffee production and consumption, including import-export dynamics, coffee prices, and overall coffee aspects and quality. For example, we were particularly interested in further exploring the differences between countries that primarily export coffee versus those that import it. Arabica / Robusta Data

The ICO Dataset, or International Coffee Organization Dataset, is a comprehensive compilation of coffee statistics worldwide. It encompasses data from various sources, providing insights into coffee consumption, production, exports, prices, and more. With 12 files and 336 columns, this dataset covers a wide range of aspects related to the coffee industry, spanning from 1990 to the present day. From disappearance and domestic consumption to exports, prices, and production, the ICO Dataset offers valuable information for researchers, industry professionals, and enthusiasts alike.

The Likeability and Harvest of Coffee Throughout the World

Harvested Bags Per Year

×

Delve into the dynamics of coffee production over time with our Harvested Bags per Year Dashboard. Utilizing data from the ICO Coffee Dataset, this dashboard allows you to explore harvest yields across different years. Simply double-tap on a year to input a specific harvest year, or toggle between years using the provided arrows. Hover over any country to see the recorded number of bags harvested in that year, providing valuable insights into the dataset's coverage and the global coffee industry's dynamics. Please note that while these numbers represent a portion of total coffee production, they offer significant insights into production trends.

Taste Profile

×

Explore the intricate flavors of coffee from around the world with our Taste Profile Dashboard. This dashboard provides insights into coffee flavor profiles sourced from the ICO Dataset. The ICO Dataset offers scores for each bag of coffee across categories like Aroma, Balance, Body, Flavor, and Sweetness, rated on a scale from 0 to 10. Use the user-friendly sliders or input specific ranges to filter the data and observe the number of bags falling within your selected parameters. Discover the origins of these coffees and gain new insights into average scores per country as you hover over each location.

Import and Export

×

This viz shows the geographical distributions of top-exporting, top-importing, and top-re-exporting countries. The size and color of bubbles represents the quantity of these measures, in units of millions of KG of coffee. Exports refer to the amount of coffee exported from a country, imports refer to the amount of coffee imported into a country, and re-exports refer to the amount of coffee re-exported after being imported into a country.
